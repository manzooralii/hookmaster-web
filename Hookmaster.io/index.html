How does it work?

1. Prepare Your Hooks

Type your hooks into Google Sheets then paste the link onto HooksMaster.io

2. Select your Content

Choose what clip you want to display on the video for the hook.

3. Select AI Voiceover

From Eleven Labs, paste your API key in and select which voice ID you want to use.

4. Customise The Hooks

Choose what color you want the hooks box and font to be.

5. Generate Your Hooks

Click "create" to generate your hooks and then download them.

6. Merge Into Creatives

Use our merging tool so you can merge your hooks onto your existing creatives.
